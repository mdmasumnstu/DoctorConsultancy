About Us

Our Free Doctor: Your Convenient Digital Healthcare Website for Patients and Doctors in Bangladesh.

Need of Our Free Doctor

For health inconvenience people often hesitate to give a visit to doctors for consultation and also waiting in queue and travelling to meet doctor make it more tedious. Need is also felt by masses to find and consult trustworthy expert doctors for their health issue and also to take second opinion related to it.

Our Free Doctor application caters to solve above mentioned problem and it is a digital Healthcare mobile app for patients and doctors in India that allow online consultation.

Through this telemedicine is not only mad easy but also accessible to wider patient best making consultation Hassle free.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our Free Doctor empowers patients across Bangladesh with instant online consultations from qualified doctors, making quality healthcare accessible and affordable, even in remote areas.

Vision

We envision a future where mobile healthcare apps revolutionize access to care, putting patients in control of their health anytime, anywhere.

Our Free Doctor For Everyone

  • In-Person Consultations: Book physical appointments with doctors through Our Free Doctor. Find specialists and schedule visits that fit your busy schedule.
  • Convenient Video/Audio Calls: Connect with qualified doctors via secure video or audio calls. Get medical advice from home, anytime.
  • Our Free Doctor Health Card: Organize and access your medical history, prescriptions, lab results, and health plans securely with Our Free Doctor health cards.
  • Online Medicine Delivery: Order medications through the app and have them delivered to your doorstep.
  • Online Lab Test Facility: Schedule lab tests directly through Our Free Doctor. Partnered with trusted facilities for a smooth process.
  • Quick Call Facility:Connect with a healthcare professional for brief consultations and quick answers through Our Free Doctor's app.

Our Approach to Healthcare at OurFreeDoctor:

Connect

OurFreeDoctor bridges the gap between patients and doctors across India. Get the care you need, anytime, anywhere, through our user-friendly app.

Trust

We prioritize building trust. OurFreeDoctor connects you with qualified doctors and provides a secure platform for your consultations and health information.

Transparency

OurFreeDoctor empowers you with transparency. See doctor profiles, reviews, and easily access your health records - all in one place.

Why use Our Free Doctor

Community

By submitting an eConsult your doctors can make sure every patient gets the right care, saving appointments for those that need them. If you can use eConsult it means you free up the phone line for those who are not able to use the internet.

Reassurance

We don't always know what is wrong with us, which is why we go to the doctors. By submitting an eConsult you give doctors all the information they need to decide on the right care for you and your family.

Speed/Access

Our Free Doctor is available to use from any device connected to the internet. There is no need to wait in a phone queue or visit the practice. It takes just 3-4 minutes to complete an eConsult and your practice will get back to you within 1 working day, if not sooner.

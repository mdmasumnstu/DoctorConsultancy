Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

       
           
                How can I book an appointment with a doctor?                

You can easily book an appointment by navigating to our 'Booking' page and selecting your desired specialty, doctor, and a convenient time slot. You can choose for either an in-person or an online consultation.

           
       
       
           
                Is the online video consultation service reliable?                

Yes, our video consultation service is highly reliable. We use a secure and encrypted platform to ensure your privacy and provide a seamless communication experience with our doctors.

           
       
       
           
                How do I know if the doctors are qualified?                

All doctors on our platform are thoroughly vetted and are registered medical practitioners with years of experience in their respective fields. You can view their credentials and specialties on their profile pages.

           
       
       
           
                What is your cancellation policy?                

Appointments can be canceled up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. For cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment, a cancellation fee may apply. Please check our terms and conditions for more details.

           
       
               
           
                Can I get a prescription through video consultation?                

Yes, if a doctor deems it medically necessary, they can issue a digital prescription following your video consultation. The prescription will be available in your patient portal for you to download or print.

           
       
               
           
                Do you offer emergency medical services?                

We are not an emergency service. For any medical emergency, please contact your local emergency services or visit the nearest hospital. Our platform is for non-emergency consultations and health advice.
